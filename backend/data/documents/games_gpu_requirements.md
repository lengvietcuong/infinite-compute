## Key Considerations When Choosing a GPU

### VRAM Requirements by Resolution

| Resolution | Minimum VRAM | Recommended VRAM | Ideal VRAM |
| ---------- | ------------ | ---------------- | ---------- |
| 1080p      | 4-6 GB       | 8 GB             | 12 GB+     |
| 1440p      | 8 GB         | 10-12 GB         | 16 GB      |
| 2160p (4K) | 10-12 GB     | 12-16 GB         | 16+ GB     |
| 4K Ultra   | 16 GB        | 20 GB+           | 24 GB      |

### Power Supply Considerations

- **RTX 4060**: 300W PSU minimum
- **RTX 4070**: 650W PSU recommended
- **RTX 4080**: 750W PSU recommended
- **RTX 4090**: 850W PSU recommended

### CPU Pairing Recommendations

To avoid bottlenecking, pair modern GPUs with:

- Budget GPU (RTX 4060): Intel i5-12400 / AMD Ryzen 5 5600X
- Mid-Range GPU (RTX 4070): Intel i7-12700K / AMD Ryzen 7 5800X3D
- High-End GPU (RTX 4080): Intel i7-13700K / AMD Ryzen 7 7800X3D or newer

### Additional Notes

1. **SSD Storage:** Most modern AAA games require SSD installation for optimal loading times (NVMe preferred)
2. **DirectX Version:** Ensure your GPU supports DirectX 12 for modern games
3. **Upscaling Technologies:** Modern games support DLSS (NVIDIA) or FSR 2/3 (AMD) for performance improvements
4. **Ray Tracing:** Not essential for gameplay but significantly increases GPU requirements
5. **Driver Updates:** Keep GPU drivers updated for optimal performance and stability
